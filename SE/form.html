Stategy

By analyzing your company’s security defences, we establish the vulnerabilities that your company might have and create a tailored security solution for your business.

Cyber Threat Intelligence

Threat Intelligence, is organized, analyzed and refined information about potential or current attacks that threaten an organization.

Cyber Risk Management

The next step in securing your business is to have a risk management plan in place to minimize, monitor and control the impact that the risks might have on your business.


1. Security Operations Centre (SOC)

We monitor, assess and defend your information security systems by doing a vulnerability and penetration testing.

2. Security Information & Event Management (SIEM)

By combining Security Information Management and Security Event Management, we provide real-time analysis of security alerts.


3. Network Operations Centre (NOC)

We monitor the physical servers, backup platforms; uninterruptible power supplies (UPS), VoIP systems, switches, routers, firewalls and storage platforms.
