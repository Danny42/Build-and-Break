Investments Strategy

Habsburg's investment strategy is both disciplined and opportunistic. We try not to forget the guidelines that have led to past successes - but we also depart from tradition to pursue compelling opportunities. Our goal is to look for quality investment opportunities that excite us.

As a first success led to a second, and then a third, we developed a deep base of relevant industry knowledge.

We generally seek companies that are strong financial performers and market leaders, led by a highly motivated management team that wants to own, as well as run, the business.

Our preference is for companies located in industries with favourable dynamics for sustainable earnings' growth which have resisted down cycles and show a compelling potential to improve margins and increase shareholder value.

Meet The Habsburg Team

The business backgrounds of our Directors and investment team are diverse and include general management, consulting, turnaround management and technologist. This diversity has been further strengthened by the knowledge gained through investing together over several business cycles.

Pedro Viudez

Founder

Pedro is an accomplished Entrepreneur and Executive. He has played a key role in building some of our key Technology, Media, and Telecommunications businesses.

Pedro founded, along with two ex-Andersen colleagues (Demetri Manias and Kris Mietz), Enterprise Outsourcing, which is a pioneer in providing “Converged” Cloud Solutions and continues to be a leading technology provider.

Pedro has been instrumental in developing niche companies such as Secured Enterprise, Finance Apps, HR Artis, CRM Apps, eNew Media, which have become successful companies within their own right.

Pedro founded Habsburg in 2000 in London, and he enjoys focusing on early-stage companies. He has demonstrated a unique understanding of what the market needs, as well as their prerequisites in software design. This assists him in guiding start-ups from concept to execution and by nurturing businesses with “network effect” and “long tail”.


Before Habsburg, he served as a partner of Arthur Andersen for 10 years, he headed up part of Andersen technology business across Europe, Middle East, India, and Africa (EMEIA). He also served as the Chief Information Officer of Tiger Brands Limited for 5 years.

Pedro was born in Cuba, grew up in Barcelona (Spain) and his primary residence is in Johannesburg, South Africa, Spain, and Australia. He has studied and furthered his education at various institutions such as Barcelona University, Harvard Business School, Kellogg’s School of Management as well as Columbia.

Beyond start-ups and technology, Pedro has completed a number of private equity investments in Consumer Goods, Manufacturing and Real Estate. Pedro provides advisory to several companies, and he continues to be a board member in many of the equity investments held by Habsburg within these industries.
